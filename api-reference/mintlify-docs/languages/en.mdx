### üìÖ Dates and Times

Control how dates, times, and temporal expressions are formatted in your translations.

**Rules available:** 3

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Calendar Era">
Choose how to represent historical periods (BC/AD vs BCE/CE).

**Available options:**
1. **`use-bc-and-ad`** - Use BC and AD for historical periods
2. **`use-bce-and-ce`** - Use BCE and CE for historical periods

</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Date Format">
Control how dates are displayed (month/day/year vs day/month/year, etc.).

**Available options:**
1. **`use-dd-slash-mm-slash-yyyy-with-leading-zeros-for-single-digit-days-and-months`** - Dd slash mm slash yyyy with leading zeros for single digit days and months
2. **`use-dd-space-spelled-out-month-space-yyyy-without-leading-zeros-for-single-digit-days`** - Dd space spelled out month space yyyy without leading zeros for single digit days
3. **`use-mm-slash-dd-slash-yyyy-with-leading-zeros-for-single-digit-days-and-months`** - Mm slash dd slash yyyy with leading zeros for single digit days and months
4. **`use-spelled-out-month-space-dd-comma-space-yyyy-without-leading-zeros-for-single-digit-days`** - Spelled out month space dd comma space yyyy without leading zeros for single digit days
5. **`use-yyyy-hyphen-mm-hyphen-dd-with-leading-zeros-for-single-digit-days-and-months`** - Yyyy hyphen mm hyphen dd with leading zeros for single digit days and months

</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Time Format">
Choose between 12-hour and 24-hour clock formats.

**Available options:**
1. **`use-12-hour-clock-and-lowercase-am-or-pm-with-periods`** - 12-hour with lowercase a.m./p.m.
2. **`use-12-hour-clock-and-lowercase-am-or-pm-with-periods-except-use-noon-and-midnight-instead-of-12-am-and-12-pm`** - 12 hour clock and lowercase am or pm with periods except noon and midnight instead of 12 am and 12 pm
3. **`use-12-hour-clock-and-lowercase-am-or-pm-without-periods`** - 12 hour clock and lowercase am or pm without periods
4. **`use-12-hour-clock-and-lowercase-am-or-pm-without-periods-except-use-noon-and-midnight-instead-of-12-am-and-12-pm`** - 12 hour clock and lowercase am or pm without periods except noon and midnight instead of 12 am and 12 pm
5. **`use-12-hour-clock-and-uppercase-am-or-pm-with-periods`** - 12 hour clock and uppercase am or pm with periods
6. **`use-12-hour-clock-and-uppercase-am-or-pm-with-periods-except-use-noon-and-midnight-instead-of-12-am-and-12-pm`** - 12 hour clock and uppercase am or pm with periods except noon and midnight instead of 12 am and 12 pm
7. **`use-12-hour-clock-and-uppercase-am-or-pm-without-periods`** - 12-hour with uppercase AM/PM
8. **`use-12-hour-clock-and-uppercase-am-or-pm-without-periods-except-use-noon-and-midnight-instead-of-12-am-and-12-pm`** - 12 hour clock and uppercase am or pm without periods except noon and midnight instead of 12 am and 12 pm
9. **`use-24-hour-clock`** - 24-hour clock format (00:00-23:59)

</Accordion>

## Language-Specific Information

English has many regional variants (US, UK, Canadian, Australian, etc.). Style rules help you choose the appropriate variant for your audience.

## Common Use Cases

- Business communications (formal tone, US/UK spelling)
- Academic writing (formal tone, specific citation styles)
- Technical documentation (clear, concise language)
- Marketing content (engaging, brand-appropriate tone)

## Getting Started

1. **Identify your needs** - What type of content are you translating?
2. **Choose relevant rules** - Focus on rules that affect your content type
3. **Test different options** - Try various combinations to find what works best
4. **Apply consistently** - Use the same rules across all your content
